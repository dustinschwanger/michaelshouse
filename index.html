A Safe Haven for Children

Michael's House
Child Advocacy Center

Michael's House provides a child friendly environment in which sensitive interviews involving physical and/or sexual abuse of children can be conducted and recorded with elaborate video equipment.

Our Goals

  • Minimize the trauma these crimes and the subsequent court proceedings inflict on the child victim and family members in their support group.
  • Increase the likelihood of conviction of the perpetrators for these offenses.
  • Provide support for child victims and family members in their support group through immediate follow-up professional counseling and medical services.
