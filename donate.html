Thank You

Your generosity enables us to provide a safe haven for children and families during their most difficult times. Together, we can ensure that every child in Erie County has access to the compassionate, professional care they deserve.

Every donation makes a difference. Thank you for supporting Michael's House.

The mission of Michael's House is to maintain a child advocacy center in Erie County that will make forensic interviews more efficient and effective by utilizing a dedicated child/family friendly site and a multi-disciplinary team of trained professionals.
